Factorial has secured $150 million in Series D funding led by General Catalyst, achieving a $2.5 billion valuation and ranking among Europe's top 20 most valuable scale-ups. The company serves over 16,000 businesses across 90 countries and is transitioning from traditional HR SaaS into an AI-powered workforce operations platform. The funding will accelerate Factorial's expansion as one of the most prominent AI-driven enterprise technology companies in Europe.
