Mosyle is a cloud-native MDM platform founded in 2012 by former Jamf employees that focuses exclusively on Apple devices including Mac, iPad, iPhone, and Apple TV, offering a free tier for up to 30 devices and three paid subscription options. Organizations seeking broader cross-platform support or advanced enterprise features may find Mosyle limiting as their device fleet expands beyond Apple's ecosystem. This article reviews seven alternative MDM solutions with different pricing structures and capabilities for teams managing mixed or non-Apple device environments.
