Hexnode, owned by Mitsogo, is a unified endpoint management (UEM) solution recognized for its accessibility and support for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, iPadOS, Android, ChromeOS, tvOS, FireOS, visionOS, and Android TV. Its per-device pricing and free trial make it popular among small and medium-sized businesses starting their fleet management journey. This article explores seven comparable platforms for organizations that need different feature sets, pricing models, or support for specialized device types.
